Indian culture is the heritage of social norms and technologies thatb orginated in or are associated with the ethno-linguistically diverse India, pertaining to the Indian subcontinent untill 1947 and the Republic of India post-1947. The term also applies beyond India to countries and cultures whose histories are strongly connected to India by immigration,colonization or influence particularly in South Asia and South East Asia.India's languages,religions,dance,music,architecture,food and customs differ from place to place within the country.
